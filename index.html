Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Slang Translator AI handles user information.

Age Restriction

This app may include slang, strong informal expressions, profanity, and adult-oriented language styles. The app is intended for users aged 16+ and is not intended for children.

Information We Process

The app may process text, voice input, and images uploaded or captured by the user to provide translation features.

Microphone Permission

Microphone access is used only to transcribe and translate spoken input.

Camera and Photo Permission

Camera or photo access is used only to read and translate visible text.

Translation and AI Processing

Text, audio, or image content may be sent to third-party AI/API services for transcription, OCR, and translation.

Local History

The app may save translation history locally on the user’s device. Users can delete history inside the app.

Account Login

Users may optionally sign in using Google Sign-In to restore purchases, sync Premium access, and improve account management. Basic account information such as email address and profile name may be processed for this purpose.

Premium Features and Payments

Some features may require payment or subscription. Purchases are processed securely through official platform providers such as Google Play. We do not store users’ payment card details.

Subscription Management

Users can manage or cancel subscriptions through their Google Play account settings. Restore Purchase may be available for previously purchased Premium access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal data. Data may only be processed by service providers required for translation, transcription, OCR, hosting, analytics, account, or payment features.
