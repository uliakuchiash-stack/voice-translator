Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

AI Slang and Business Translator (“we”, “our”, “app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Process

Our app may process the following types of information:

  • Text entered manually by the user
  • Voice input recorded by the user for speech-to-text translation
  • Photos uploaded by the user for photo translation and text recognition
  • Translation preferences (language, style, tone, slang settings)
  • Basic device information required for app functionality

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Microphone Permission

The app may request access to your microphone only when you choose to use voice translation or live dialogue mode.

Microphone access is used only to process your speech for transcription and translation. We do not record audio in the background.

3. Camera Permission

The app may request access to your camera only when you choose to use Photo Translate or scan text from an image.

Camera access is used only for:

  • Taking photos for translation
  • Scanning documents or text
  • Extracting text from images (OCR)

The app does not access your camera without your action.

4. Image Processing

Photos uploaded for translation are processed only for:

  • Text recognition (OCR)
  • Translation of the detected text

Images are not sold, shared for advertising purposes, or permanently stored beyond what is necessary for processing.

5. AI and Third-Party Processing

To provide high-quality translation, text, voice, and image data may be temporarily processed by trusted third-party AI providers such as OpenAI and OCR service providers.

These providers process data only for delivering translation and language assistance features.

6. Internet Access

The app requires internet access to:

  • Process AI translations
  • Convert speech to text
  • Generate business emails and messages
  • Perform photo translation and OCR

7. Data Storage

Some translation history may be stored locally on your device for convenience and faster access.

We do not permanently store private conversations on our servers unless technically required for temporary processing.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
