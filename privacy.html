Age Restriction

This app may include slang, strong informal expressions, profanity, and adult-oriented language styles. The app is intended for users aged 16+ and is not intended for children.

Age Confirmation

By using this app, users confirm that they are 16 years old or older according to local legal requirements.

Information We Process

The app may process text, voice input, and images uploaded or captured by the user in order to provide translation features.

Microphone Permission

The app may request microphone access so users can speak phrases for voice translation. Audio is used only to transcribe and translate spoken input.

Camera and Photo Permission

The app may request camera or photo access so users can take or upload images containing text for photo translation. Images are used only to read and translate visible text.

Translation and AI Processing

Text, audio, or image content may be sent to third-party AI/API services in order to provide transcription, OCR, and translation functionality. These services process the content only for the purpose of generating the requested translation.

Local History

The app may save translation history locally on the user’s device for convenience. Users can delete their translation history using the Delete History option inside the app.

Premium Features

Some features of the app may require payment or subscription. Purchases are processed securely through official platform providers such as Google Play and Apple App Store. We do not store users’ payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal data. We do not share user data with advertisers or data brokers. Data may only be processed by service providers required to operate translation, transcription, OCR, hosting, analytics, or payment features.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access to connect with translation and AI services.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete it where required.
