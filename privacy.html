Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

2. Microphone Access

Our app requests microphone permission only to provide voice transcription and voice translation features. Microphone access is used only when you actively choose to use voice input.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for:

  • Speech recognition and transcription

4. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your voice recordings. Voice and text data are processed securely for translation purposes only. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use secure third-party AI and translation services to process translations and improve response quality. These services are used only for providing app functionality.

6. Your Control

You can:

  • Disable microphone access anytime in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
